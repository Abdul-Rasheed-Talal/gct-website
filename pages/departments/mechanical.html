About This Program

The Mechanical Technology department offers comprehensive training in machine design, manufacturing processes, thermal engineering, and industrial maintenance.

Students learn CAD/CAM technologies, CNC programming, industrial robotics, and gain hands-on experience with advanced mechanical systems and precision engineering techniques.
