About This Program

The Computer Information Technology department focuses on cutting-edge software development, network management, cybersecurity, and advanced computing technologies.

Students receive hands-on training in programming languages, database management, web development, and emerging tech trends. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for the rapidly evolving IT industry.
