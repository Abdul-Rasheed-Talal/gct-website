About This Program

Electronics Technology program focuses on advanced electronic systems, circuit design, telecommunications, and embedded systems.

Students develop expertise in analog and digital electronics, microcontrollers, signal processing, IoT technologies, and receive practical training in electronic device fabrication and testing.
